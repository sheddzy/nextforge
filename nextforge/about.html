Nextforge Academy was founded on one belief: Nigerians deserve world-class tech training that actually gets them hired.

Our Mission

Closing the Gap Between Ambition and Opportunity

Too many talented Nigerians have the drive and intelligence to build careers in tech — but lack access to quality, practical training that connects to real jobs.

Nextforge Academy was built to change that. Every programme is designed around one question: "Will this get our students hired?" Not just trained. Not just certified. Hired.

We run live classes taught by active professionals, assign real-world projects that build genuine portfolios, and provide career support that doesn't end at graduation.
