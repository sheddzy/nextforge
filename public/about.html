Our Mission

To Make High-Quality Tech Training Accessible to Anyone, Anywhere.

The global tech skills gap is one of the most significant barriers to economic mobility for young professionals in Africa and beyond. Millions of talented people cannot access the structured, industry-aligned training they need to compete in the modern economy.

NextForge Academy was founded to bridge that gap. We design every programme around the realities of working professionals — people juggling jobs, families, and financial pressures who still have the ambition to build something better for themselves.

Our goal is not just to teach skills. It is to change economic trajectories. A graduate who secures a higher-paying role, wins a freelance client, or launches a business has demonstrably improved their life.

Our Vision

The Most Trusted Tech Academy for Ambitious Professionals in the Global South.

We are building NextForge into the go-to name for practical, mentor-led tech education across Africa, the Caribbean, Southeast Asia, and beyond — everywhere that ambition exceeds access.

Within five years, we aim to train 50,000 professionals, operate in 30 countries, and establish employer partnerships that create direct career pipelines for our graduates.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Four principles that guide every decision we make at NextForge Academy.

🔩

Practicality First

No fluff. Every lesson must be immediately applicable in a real job or freelance context. If it doesn't translate to work, it doesn't make the curriculum.

🌍

Globally Minded

Built with African market realities at the core, but designed to serve professionals anywhere in the world who want real skills, not credentials.

🤝

Community-Led

Small cohorts create real accountability. Our student community remains active long after graduation — this isn't a one-and-done transaction.

📈

Career-Driven

Every programme is designed to unlock a promotion, a new role, or a paying client. We track outcomes, not just completions.
