Common Questions

Everything You Need to Know

Can't find your answer here? WhatsApp us at +234 906 091 4286 or email info@nextforgeacademy.online

Do I need any prior experience to enroll?+
No prior experience is required for most of our programmes. Our Project Management, Data Analysis, Product Management, and Business Communication courses are all designed to take complete beginners to job-ready. The Operations Systems Design course is recommended for intermediate learners.
How are classes delivered?+
All classes are delivered live via video conferencing. Every session is recorded and uploaded to your student portal within 24 hours, so you never miss a lesson. Classes are scheduled around working hours to accommodate professionals in different time zones.
Can I join from outside Nigeria?+
Absolutely. We welcome students from anywhere in the world. We have students from Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya, South Africa, the UK, Canada, and more. Our pricing is in Naira but we accept international card payments through Paystack. Please contact us for international payment options if needed.
What is your refund policy?+
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you complete less than 20% of the course content and are unsatisfied within the first 30 days, we will issue a full refund. Please contact us at info@nextforgeacademy.online to initiate a refund request.
How long until I get a job after completing a course?+
Results vary based on your background and job search effort, but many of our graduates have secured roles within 1–4 months of completing their programme. We provide CV reviews, interview preparation, LinkedIn optimisation, and job application support as part of every course.
Are certificates internationally recognised?+
Our certificates are issued with a unique verification code that employers can verify on our website. Certificates are accepted by organisations across Africa, the UK, and beyond. We are actively pursuing additional accreditation partnerships.
What time commitment is required per week?+
Expect to commit 8–12 hours per week. This includes live class time (2–3 hours), self-study and recordings (3–4 hours), and assignments (2–4 hours). Our programmes are designed to be manageable alongside a full-time job.
Is there payment in installments?+
We are exploring installment options for future cohorts. Currently, full payment is required at enrollment. However, we offer early-bird pricing for the first 5 students who enroll in each cohort. Contact our admissions team on WhatsApp to ask about any available discounts.
