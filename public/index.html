Common Questions

Everything You Need to Know

Can't find your answer? WhatsApp us: +234 906 091 4286

Do I need any prior experience?+
No prior experience required for most programmes. PM, Data Analysis, Product Management, and Business Communication are all designed for complete beginners.
How are classes delivered?+
All classes are delivered live via video conferencing. Every session is recorded and uploaded to your student portal within 24 hours.
Can I join from outside Nigeria?+
Absolutely. We have students from Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya, South Africa, the UK, Canada and more. We accept international card payments through Paystack.
What is your refund policy?+
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you complete less than 20% of the course content and are unsatisfied, we'll issue a full refund.
How long until I get a job?+
Many graduates secure roles within 1–4 months. We provide CV reviews, interview prep, LinkedIn optimisation and job search support in every programme.
What time commitment is needed per week?+
Expect 8–12 hours per week: live class (2–3hrs), recordings/study (3–4hrs), and assignments (2–4hrs). Designed to run alongside a full-time job.
Is there an installment payment option?+
Full payment is currently required at enrollment. Early-bird discounts apply to the first 5 students per cohort. Contact our admissions team for current offers.
